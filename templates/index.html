6. Quantitative Methodology Reference

01

Feature Engineering

Calculates 6 mathematical metrics: Daily Log Return, 21d Volatility (Annualized), 21d Mean Return, 63d Momentum, RSI-14, and the 50d/200d Moving Average Ratio.

02

PCA & Clustering

Standardizes indicators with `StandardScaler`, applies PCA, and clusters the space into $K=3$ groups. Centroids are dynamically ranked by 21d mean return to designate 🔴 Bear, 🟡 Sideways, and 🟢 Bull regimes.

03

Ward Linkage

Daily indicators are downsampled to monthly frequency using resample("ME"). Agglomerative Hierarchical clustering utilizes the Ward variance-minimization linkage to explore macro trends.
