7. Quantitative Methodology Reference

01

Composite Features

Daily indicators are calculated for 8 major Indian stocks: Log return, Volatility, Mean return, Momentum, RSI-14, and MA ratio. These features are then **averaged** to create a unified composite matrix.

02

Clustering & PCA

Scale composite features via StandardScaler. PCA reduces dimensionality for plotting. K-Means fits for $K=3$. Centroids are dynamically sorted by annualized returns to map Bear, Sideways, and Bull regimes.

03

Monthly Ward Linkage

Composite daily data is downsampled using resample("ME"). Agglomerative Hierarchical clustering applies **Ward linkage** on the resampled monthly data to partition long-term cycles.
